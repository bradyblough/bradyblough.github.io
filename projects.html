Kepler's Third Law Calculator

A web app designed to compute the orbital period of planets around different stars. Input the planet's semi-major axis and the central star's mass, and the app calculates the orbital period in both years and days.

GitHub Demo

Cosmological Redshift Simulator

An application that visually illustrates the astronomical concept of redshift. Users can manipulate a slider to increase the redshift, causing a line to dynamically extend across the canvas. The line starts as a deep blue and gradually transitions to a vibrant red, mimicking the color shift observed in astrophysical phenomena.

GitHub Demo
